CAMBRIDGE RESORT

With its first-rate amenities and low rates, Cambridge Resort is considered one of the best resorts in Cavite, Philippines. We offer guests with endless opportunities to relax, unwind, and have fun. No matter your age, we guarantee that you will make the most out of your stay in our hotel and resort. You will most likely remember the days you’ve spent here for the rest of your life.

Conveniently located in Rosario, Cavite, we are just an hour away from the city. Our clean, spacious, and lovely resort is equipped with beautifully furnished amenities, including our grand hotel and swimming pools. We have prepared various leisure activities for everyone, from the most laid-back individuals to tourists who seek for a different kind of thrill and businessmen looking for inspiration for new work ideas. Drive your way to our resort in the South and experience the splendid wonders that await you!
